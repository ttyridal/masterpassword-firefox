New data format

This version of MasterPassword changes the internal representation for sites. This is done for a couple of reasons. First, the export file can only represent a sitename once. The addon could previously have a sitename with different configurations registered on different websites (ie. sitename google.com could have different generation number when visiting google.co.uk and google.com).

Furthermore, the new representation is better suited for working around storage limitations imposed by the browser.

Please head over to the configuration view to execute the upgrade.

Privacy Policy

Neither Masterpassword nor it's authors collect any information from or about the usage of Masterpassword.
full stop!

Masterpassword store your settings (site name, etc) locally on your device. This information is never transferred anywhere by Masterpassword.

Your browser vendor may collect data on your usage of this extension; when you download it, every time you activate it (and site was active when you did) etc. Please refer to your vendor's policy for details.
Further, if you have enabled synchronization of addon-data within your browser, the browser may transfer and store this on a synchronization server. Again please refer to your browser vendor's policy for details.
